Michael Jackson Life summary

Michael Jackson was born on August 29, 1958, in Gary, Indiana, and was the eighth of ten children in his family. He began his music career as a child, singing with his brothers in the group, The Jackson 5. In the 1980s, he achieved global fame as a solo artist with hit albums such as "Thriller" and "Bad." Throughout his life, Jackson faced numerous controversies, including accusations of child sexual abuse, financial difficulties, and public scrutiny of his changing appearance. He died on June 25, 2009, at the age of 50.
